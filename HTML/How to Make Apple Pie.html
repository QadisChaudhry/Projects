How to Make Apple Pie

Ingredients

    Dough:

  • 2 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons of sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoons of fine salt
  • 14 tablespoons of cold butter,diced
  • 1 large egg, lighly beaten with 2 tablespoons of cold water

    • Filling:

  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 3 ponds baking apples
  • 2/3 cup sugar, plus more for sprinkling on the pie
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Generous pinch or ground nutmeg
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 425DF. Prepare double-crust pastery
  2. Mix sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt in large bowl. Stir in apples
  3. Roll other round of pastery. Fold into fourths and cut slits so steam can escape.
  4. Bake 40 to 50 minutes or until crust is brown and juice begins to bubble through slits in the crust
